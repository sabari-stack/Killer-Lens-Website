Questions
Answered.

Everything you need to know before booking a session with us.

Ask us directly

How far in advance should I book a session?

Book at least 2to4 weeks in advance.

What should I wear to my portrait session?

Wear comfortable clothes with simple colors.

How long does it take to receive my images?

You will receive edited photos within 7to10 days.

Do you shoot on location or only in the studio?

We provide both studio and outdoor photography.

Can I use my images for commercial purposes?

Yes, with prior permission and according to the package.

What happens if I need to reschedule?

Please inform us at least 48 hours before your session.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, they are available as an additional service.

How many people can I bring to my session?

Family and group sessions are available depending on the package.
